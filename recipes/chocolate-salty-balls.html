Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls Recipe

Description

Despite the obvious metaphor here, I thought a salty chocolate pastry sounded pretty darn good... and guess what? I was right! Chef's balls are delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup salted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 4 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • Coarse sea salt, to taste

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, bring the butter, sugar and brandy to a boil, stirring constantly.
  2. Remove from the heat. Stir (with a wooden spoon) in 2 cups of chocolate chips until melted.
  3. Remember to give that spoon a lick! Cool mixture slightly.
  4. Stir in vanilla. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Gradually add the chocolate mixture and mix well.
  5. Combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt, then gradually add the dry ingredients to the chocolate mixture.
  6. Stir in any remaining chocolate chips. Spread into a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake at 325 degrees for 35-45 minutes or until a fork inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Careful not to burn your balls! Let cool just enough that it won't burn your hands.
  9. Meanwhile set up a small plate or cutting board with some of the sea salt on it.
  10. Scrape up the brownie mix, working around the edges. You don't want to use the hard edges for the balls.

  12. Do this very lightly (I suggest you do NOT try to emulate the picture), a little sea salt goes a very long way.
  13. If you do happen to oversalt, just brush the excess salt crystals off. Too much salt is bad, mmmkay?
  14. You're done, put 'em in your mouth and suck 'em!
