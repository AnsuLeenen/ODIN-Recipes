Lembas Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 c. honey
  • 3 kumquats, whole (can substitute orange zest and a bit of juice if desired)
  • 2 tsp. orange flower or rose water (optional)
  • 3 oz. chopped almonds or macadamia nuts (fruits of the Mallorn tree)
  • ¼ c. melted butter
  • 2 ¼ c. flour (barley flour of you want to be really accurate)
  • ½ tsp. salt

Directions

  1. Put the eggs, butter, honey, kumquats, rose or orange flower water, and nuts in a food processor or blender.
  2. Blend on high for 2-4 minutes. Add 1 cup of the flour. Blend for a minute or two.
  3. Put mixture into a bowl and add the remaining flour and the salt. Whisk or stir until well blended.
  4. Bake a small amount of dough (about two tablespoons) at a time on a pizzelle or iron about 15 seconds or until lightly brown, for a flat bread like texture.
  5. They can also be baked at 350 degrees for about 10-15 minutes. Wrap in a leaf and tie with a string!
